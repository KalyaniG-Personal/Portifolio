About Me

Engineer by craft.
Architect by vision.

I'm Kalyani Guggilla — a Data & Integration Engineer who believes that strong data infrastructure is the backbone of every intelligent business. Over 4+ years at Commonwealth Care Alliance in Boston, I designed and delivered the ETL pipelines, cloud integrations, and enterprise data workflows that real organizations relied on every day.

My toolkit spans Talend, SSIS, Azure Data Factory, Teradata, and SQL — but what defines my approach goes beyond the tools. I think in systems: where data originates, how it moves, where it breaks, and how to make it trustworthy at scale.

"I don't just move data. I make it trustworthy, timely, and actionable — and increasingly, intelligent."

I'm currently deepening my expertise with a Master of Science in Artificial Intelligence (Data Analytics) at Indiana Wesleyan University (Class of 2027) — positioning myself at the intersection of robust data engineering and the next wave of AI-driven analytics.
